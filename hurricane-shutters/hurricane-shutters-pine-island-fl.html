Our Hurricane Shutter Solutions

Accordion Hurricane Shutters

Accordion hurricane shutters offer the perfect combination of convenience and protection for Pine Island homeowners. These permanently installed shutters fold neatly against your home's exterior when not in use, maintaining your property's aesthetic appeal while providing instant storm protection when needed.

Key Benefits:

  • Quick and easy deployment - simply unfold and lock
  • Permanently installed for instant availability
  • Heavy-duty aluminum construction meets Florida Building Code
  • Minimal storage requirements
  • Ideal for frequently used windows and sliding glass doors

Aluminum Rolling Shutters

For the ultimate in convenience and protection, aluminum rolling shutters represent the premium choice for Pine Island residents. These motorized shutters roll up into a compact housing box above your windows, remaining completely hidden when not deployed.

Key Benefits:

  • Motorized operation - push-button convenience
  • Completely hidden when not in use
  • Perfect for multi-story homes and hard-to-reach windows
  • Superior impact resistance
  • Battery backup systems available for power outages

Bahama Shutters

Bahama shutters bring tropical elegance to storm protection, making them a popular choice among Pine Island homeowners who want year-round aesthetic appeal. These top-hinged shutters mount above your windows and provide shade, privacy, and ventilation while adding distinctive architectural character.

Key Benefits:

  • Year-round aesthetic appeal and curb appeal
  • Provides shade and reduces cooling costs
  • Quick fold-down storm protection
  • Available in multiple colors and styles
  • Louvered design allows airflow and natural light

Colonial Shutters

Colonial shutters offer timeless architectural appeal while delivering robust storm protection for your Pine Island home. These side-hinged shutters mount beside your windows and swing closed to meet in the middle, creating a classic look that complements traditional home designs.

Key Benefits:

  • Timeless architectural elegance
  • Increases property value and curb appeal
  • Available in solid panel or louvered styles
  • Customizable colors to match your home
  • Easy operation with secure locking
