Accordion Hurricane Shutters

Accordion hurricane shutters represent one of the most popular and practical choices for Weston homeowners seeking reliable storm protection. These shutters feature a unique folding design that allows them to be easily deployed when a storm approaches and neatly stored when not in use.

Key Benefits:

  • Easy deployment without professional assistance
  • Heavy-duty aluminum construction for exceptional impact resistance
  • Permanent installation - no storage needed
  • Energy cost savings through extra insulation
  • Enhanced home security year-round

Aluminum Rolling Shutters

Aluminum rolling shutters offer the ultimate in convenience and protection for discerning Weston homeowners. These premium shutters operate with the simple push of a button, rolling up and down smoothly within their housing units mounted above windows and doors.

Key Benefits:

  • Motorized operation for effortless control
  • Ideal for multi-story homes
  • Superior storm protection with robust construction
  • Virtually invisible when retracted
  • Year-round privacy and light control

Bahama Shutters

Bahama shutters bring a distinctive tropical aesthetic to Weston homes while delivering reliable hurricane protection. These shutters are hinged at the top and propped open at an angle, creating an attractive awning effect that adds character and charm to any architectural style.

Key Benefits:

  • Tropical aesthetic enhances curb appeal
  • Natural light filtering with shade and privacy
  • Quick deployment for storm protection
  • Reduces cooling costs by blocking direct sunlight
  • Available in multiple colors and finishes

Colonial Shutters

Colonial shutters offer timeless elegance combined with practical storm protection, making them perfect for Weston homes with traditional or colonial architectural styles. These classic shutters feature a traditional divided-panel design that adds sophisticated charm to any home's exterior.
