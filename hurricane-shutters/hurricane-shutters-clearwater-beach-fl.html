Our Hurricane Shutter Solutions

Accordion Hurricane Shutters

Accordion hurricane shutters offer the perfect combination of convenience and robust protection for Clearwater Beach residents. These permanently mounted shutters fold neatly against your home's exterior when not in use, maintaining your property's aesthetic appeal while remaining instantly accessible when storms threaten. Constructed from heavy-duty aluminum, accordion shutters provide excellent wind resistance and impact protection, meeting or exceeding Florida's strict building codes. Their user-friendly design allows homeowners to quickly deploy protection across windows and doors without requiring additional storage space or complex installation procedures during emergency preparations.

Aluminum Rolling Shutters

Aluminum rolling shutters represent the pinnacle of hurricane protection technology, offering motorized convenience at the touch of a button. These premium shutters roll up into a compact housing unit above your windows and doors, remaining completely hidden when retracted. When activated, they provide superior protection against high winds, flying debris, and forced entry attempts. Many Clearwater Beach homeowners appreciate the added benefits of enhanced privacy, noise reduction, and energy efficiency that rolling shutters provide year-round. Available in both manual and motorized configurations, these shutters can be integrated with smart home systems for ultimate convenience and security.

Bahama Shutters

Bahama shutters bring tropical elegance to hurricane protection, making them an ideal choice for Clearwater Beach's coastal aesthetic. These hinged shutters mount above windows and doors, providing continuous shade and architectural charm while standing ready for storm deployment. When hurricane conditions approach, Bahama shutters quickly fold down and secure in place, offering reliable protection without compromising your home's visual appeal. Beyond storm protection, these shutters help reduce cooling costs by blocking direct sunlight, provide enhanced privacy, and add distinctive curb appeal that complements Florida's relaxed coastal lifestyle.

Colonial Shutters

Colonial shutters combine timeless architectural beauty with modern hurricane protection technology, perfect for homeowners who value both style and safety. These traditional-looking shutters feature a classic panel design that enhances your property's curb appeal while providing dependable storm protection. Unlike decorative shutters, Storm Smart's colonial shutters are fully functional, engineered to withstand hurricane-force winds and impact from windborne debris.
