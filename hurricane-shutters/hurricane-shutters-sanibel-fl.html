Our Hurricane Shutter Solutions

Accordion Hurricane Shutters

Accordion hurricane shutters are among the most popular and practical protection solutions for Sanibel homeowners. These shutters feature a folding design that allows them to accordion out from either side of your windows and doors, providing complete coverage when deployed. When not in use, they fold neatly against the sides of your openings, maintaining your home's aesthetic appeal while remaining ready for quick deployment.

Key Benefits:

  • Easy one-person operation in minutes
  • Permanent installation - no storage needed
  • High-grade aluminum construction
  • Withstands hurricane-force winds
  • Year-round security against break-ins

Aluminum Rolling Shutters

For the ultimate in convenience and protection, aluminum rolling shutters represent the premium choice for Sanibel properties. These shutters operate on a smooth rolling system, either manually with a crank or automatically with the push of a button, making storm preparation effortless for homeowners of all ages and physical capabilities.

Key Benefits:

  • Manual or motorized operation options
  • Discreet housing preserves curb appeal
  • Excellent insulation properties
  • Reduces energy costs year-round
  • Superior noise reduction and privacy

Bahama Shutters

Bahama shutters combine functional hurricane protection with distinctive tropical style, making them an ideal choice for Sanibel's coastal aesthetic. These hinged shutters mount above windows and prop open at an angle, providing shade and ventilation during normal weather while adding architectural charm to your home's exterior.

Key Benefits:

  • Tropical coastal aesthetic
  • Provides shade and ventilation when open
  • Quick storm deployment
  • Available in multiple colors
  • Enhances home's architectural appeal

Colonial Shutters

Colonial shutters offer timeless elegance combined with modern hurricane protection, perfect for Sanibel homes with traditional or classic architectural styles. These hinged shutters are permanently mounted beside each window and fold inward to meet in the middle, creating a secure barrier against wind and debris.

Key Benefits:

  • Classic, timeless design
  • Enhances property value
  • Multiple color options available
  • Decorative hardware choices
  • Durable aluminum construction
